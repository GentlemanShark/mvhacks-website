What is MVHacks?
MVHacks is a high school hackathon organized by Mountain View High School students. Experienced and first-time programmers will gather for 24 hours to turn their ideas into realities.

FAQ
What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is an event in which people come together to brainstorm, design, and code projects. The Googleplex venue, like-minded peers, experienced mentors, and informative seminars at MVHacks provide the tools you need to focus and succeed.
How much does it cost?
It is completely free! The food, drinks, swag, etc. are all included.
Do I need programming experience?
No - this is a fantastic opportunity to learn from experienced hackers! There will also be workshops and seminars for first-time programmers. However, we do recommend having at least one person on your team who has prior experience.
Who can attend?
All high school students are eligible.
What do I bring?
All you need is your school ID, your laptop, and your charger. In addition to this you can bring your sleeping bag if you would like. Remember to install all the software that you will need.
Do I need a team?
You can come with a team if you would like but a team is not needed. If you would like to work in a team, you can find team members at the hackathon.
What are the rules?
Be nice, respectful, and have fun!
