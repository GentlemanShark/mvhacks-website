FAQ
What is a hackathon?
There is a lot of misinformation roaming around about the meaning of the word "hacking". Though it sounds nefarious, hacking is actually completely legal and even beneficial to the digital world as a whole. Hacking is simply a synonym for programming. For instance, the IT guy at your office is a hacker. The CTO of Google is a hacker. And even the creator of Angry Birds is a hacker.
What is MVHacks?
MVHacks invites high school students to hack in the positive sense. We encourage invention and innovation by providing the resources for teams of young programmers to work together to turn their ideas into realities. MVHacks creates an environment conducive to productivity and creativity by providing a venue, like-minded peers, and experienced mentors to ensure that every student has access to all the tools they need to focus and succeed.
What do I bring?
All you need is your school ID, your laptop, and your charger. In addition to this you can bring your sleeping bag if you would like. Remember to install all the software that you will need.
Do I need a team?
You can come with a team if you would like but a team is not needed. If you would like to work in a team, you can find team member at the hackathon.
What are the rules?
Be nice, respectful, and have fun!
Who can attend?
All high school students are eligible.
How much does it cost?
It is completely FREE!
