What is MVHacks?
What is a Hackathon?

Before hacking came to mean illegal digital breaking-and-entering, it meant tinkering and creating. build on this traditional form of hacking and encourage invention and innovation by encouraging teams of young programmers to work together to build ideas into realities. Hackathons provide an environment that works to encourage this productivity and creativity by providing a venue, like-minded peers, and experienced mentors to ensure that every student has access to all the tools they need to focus and succeed. MVHacks is a hackathon organized by students at Mountain View High School (Mountain View, CA) for other high school students. It is being hosted by (VENUE HERE) and made possible through the contributions of countless sponsors and mentors. Educational seminars on (TOPICS) will help new and old hackers alike learn and develop their skills, our mentors will help teams refine and implement their ideas, and our judges will recognize and reward outstanding creations.

FAQ
What do I bring?

All you need is your school ID, your laptop, and your charger. In addition to this you can bring your sleeping bag if you would like. Remember to install all the software that you will need.

Do I need a team?

You can come with a team if you would like but a team is not needed. If you would like to work in a team, you can find team member at the hackathon.

What are the rules?

Be nice, respectful, and have fun!

How much does it cost?

It is completely FREE!
